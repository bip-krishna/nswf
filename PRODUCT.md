# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Donors, volunteers, community members, journalists, and partner organizations visiting the NSWF website to learn about the foundation's noise pollution awareness and environmental conservation work in Pune, India. Visitors range from local community members to international media outlets seeking credible information about the foundation's impact.

## Product Purpose

Nisarg Srishti Welfare Foundation's website exists to communicate the organization's mission, build trust with potential donors and partners, showcase measurable impact, and convert visitors into supporters. Success means visitors understand the urgency of noise pollution awareness and feel compelled to contribute, volunteer, or partner with the foundation.

## Brand Personality

Authoritative yet warm. Mission-driven, grassroots-focused, professionally optimistic. The brand speaks with expert confidence about environmental issues while remaining approachable and grounded in community action.

## Anti-references

Generic NGO templates with flat colors, stock photography, and templated layouts. Overly cheerful or sanitized non-profit aesthetics that feel disconnected from the real environmental challenges being addressed. Busy, cluttered layouts that undermine credibility.

## Design Principles

1. **Ground in evidence, not sentiment.** Impact stats, real sessions, certifications, and media features build trust better than emotional appeals.
2. **Show, don't tell.** Photography of real sessions, noise monitoring in action, and community engagement carries the story.
3. **Cinematic restraint.** Dark editorial aesthetic with generous whitespace — the gravity of the subject matter earns the seriousness of the design.
4. **One clear action per page.** Every page has a primary conversion (donate, contact, learn more) without competing priorities.
5. **Inclusive by default.** WCAG AA contrast, reduced-motion support, and semantic HTML are non-negotiable for a public-service organization.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum across all surfaces. Reduced motion supported throughout. High contrast text on all backgrounds is critical for an organization serving diverse communities including seniors and persons with disabilities affected by noise pollution.
