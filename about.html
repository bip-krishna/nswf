Our foundation

Vision & Mission

Nisarg Srishti Welfare Foundation (NSWF) is a Pune-based non-profit organization dedicated to creating a peaceful and sustainable society through awareness and community action. Established with the vision of creating a peaceful and sustainable world, the foundation works on social causes that directly impact the well-being of individuals and communities.

The flagship mission focuses on reducing noise pollution, a modern-day environmental challenge that is often overlooked but deeply affects health, productivity, and quality of life.

Vision

A peaceful, healthy, and environmentally responsible India where every citizen values harmony with nature and human well-being.

Mission

To work towards human welfare, women empowerment, environmental conservation, education, culture, and literature through awareness, education, and community action.

Founder

Dr. Garima Kavathekar

Dr. Garima Kavathekar is the Founder & Director of Nisarg Srishti Welfare Foundation. A TEDx speaker, award-winning social worker, and advocate for sustainable living, she leads the foundation with a clear mission — to build a society where harmony with nature and respect for human well-being go hand in hand.

Her work has been recognized internationally, with NSWF's initiatives featured by BBC World Service on People Fixing the World and in a France 24 documentary on noise pollution in India.
