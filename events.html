Events & Campaigns

NGO Activities & Events

We are working on reducing noise pollution in India and creating a happy and healthy environment for all living beings. Our vision is to reduce noise pollution and create a healthy environment for living beings.

School & community programs

Awareness Campaigns

Samata Vidyalaya, Uruli Devachi

10 January 2026

Noise Pollution Awareness Workshop conducted at Samata Vidyalaya, Uruli Devachi, run by Sane Guruji Shikshan Sanstha. The program was led by Mr. Rajendra Kulkarni and Dr. Garima Kavathekar, educating students on the harmful effects of noise pollution on health and society.

Dhyan Prabodhini School, Pune

2025

Noise Pollution awareness campaign at Dnyan Prabodhini Madhyamik and Kanishtha Mahavidyalaya. Over 100 students participated in a competition showcasing creativity and concern for the environment. First, second, and third prizes were awarded with gifts and certificates.

Vardhaman English Medium School

2025

Noise Pollution awareness campaign organized by Event Manager Shri Rajendra Kulkarni. Mrs. Neena Singh delivered an engaging talk. Supported by Principal Mrs. Sonali Malani with 80 students participating. Mr. Somesh Prem of Green Life Foundation joined the event.

Sane Guruji School, Pune

2025

'Control Honk' campaign conducted at Sane Guruji Shikshan Sanstha, Pragati Vidyalaya. Dr. Garima Kavathekar led an awareness session on health effects of noise pollution. Mr. Rajendra Kulkarni and Jadhav ji shared information on water conservation and NGO work. Over 100 students participated.

PDEA English Medium School, Akurdi

13 March 2024

Environmental awareness and women's wellness session hosted by Principal Mrs. Priti Dabade. Dr. Garima Kavathekar addressed noise pollution, plastic pollution and women's health. TEDx speaker Kushal Khemani demonstrated t-shirt to bag repurposing. Shri Rajendra Kulkarni shared information on NGO projects.

Kalawati Dattatray Kotwal High School

12 July 2025

Executive Team visit focusing on educating students about harmful effects of noise pollution and encouraging eco-friendly practices through interactive sessions, demonstrations and engaging discussions.

PMC's Late Ramchandra Bankar School

05 August 2025

Noise Pollution Awareness Campaign engaging with students, teachers and staff about the growing concern of noise pollution in urban areas and its harmful effects on health, learning and well-being.

Real-Time Noise Measurement (Ganesh Utsav 2025)

September 2025

Students from Gokhale Institute of Politics and Economics, Pune collaborated with NSWF to measure noise levels at key locations across Pune during Ganesh Utsav 2025 celebrations. Findings contribute to promoting responsible noise levels during festivals.

Milestones

Special Events

First Regional Office Inauguration

14 December 2024

The first regional office of NSWF was inaugurated in Pune, Maharashtra. The ceremony was attended by dignitaries, community leaders, and volunteers. The office features eco-friendly infrastructure designed to reduce its carbon footprint and serve as a model for sustainable architecture. The event featured interactive sessions about upcoming projects and how to get involved.

Noise Pollution & E-Waste Awareness Drive

January 2026

Program at Gyanprabodhini College in collaboration with Electrofine Recycling Pvt. Ltd., reaching over 100 students with information about scientific e-waste disposal and noise pollution awareness.

GIPE Student Visit to NSWF Office

23 August 2025

Second-year students from Gokhale Institute of Politics and Economics visited NSWF office as part of academic exposure. Students interacted with Dr. Garima Kavathekar about environmental awareness initiatives.

Foundation Day & Independence Day

2024

Celebrated Foundation Day and Independence Day at an orphanage home, spreading joy and environmental awareness among children.
