Timeline

Our Journey

Jan 2023

Foundation Established

Nisarg Srishti Welfare Foundation is registered in Pune under the leadership of Dr. Garima Kavathekar.

Feb 2024

First Major School Program

Over 100 students participate in a noise pollution competition at Sane Guruji Shikshan Sanstha, Pragati Vidyalaya. Rainwater harvesting system also evaluated.

Feb 2025

MoU with Ecoserve Development

Signed a memorandum of understanding to collaborate on noise pollution reduction, awareness campaigns, and sustainability initiatives.

Sep 2025

Ganesh Utsav Noise Monitoring

Collaborated with Gokhale Institute of Politics and Economics (GIPE) for real-time noise measurement across Pune during the festival.

Jan 2026

E-Waste & Noise Awareness Drive

Conducted awareness program at Gyanprabodhini College in collaboration with Electrofine Recycling Pvt. Ltd., reaching over 100 students.

2026

International Media Recognition

Featured by BBC World Service on People Fixing the World and in a France 24 documentary on noise pollution in India.
