Our Focus Areas

What We Do

☬

Noise Pollution Awareness

School workshops, community sessions, and expert talks across India educating about the harmful effects of noise pollution on health and well-being.

☰

Real-Time Noise Monitoring

Collecting on-ground decibel data during festivals, traffic hours, and public events to support research, advocacy, and policy change.

♺

Environmental Education

Creating documentaries, informational videos, and training modules to build an eco-conscious society through knowledge and awareness.

☾

Women Empowerment

Providing platforms for training, education, and leadership development so women can drive environmental change in their communities.
