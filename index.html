Creating a Peaceful,
Healthy & Noise-Free India

At NSWF, we work to create a healthier, peaceful, and environmentally responsible society. Our initiatives focus on reducing noise pollution, building community awareness, and empowering citizens to adopt sustainable habits.

Noise pollution facts

dB measures sound intensity level Normal conversation — 60 dB Sounds above 85 dB are harmful WHO limit: 55 dB daytime WHO night limit: 40 dB Airports often exceed 100 dB Headphones can reach 110 dB dB scale is logarithmic, not linear Prolonged 70+ dB causes hearing damage
