Membership Tiers

01

Student Member

For students enrolled in any recognized educational institution. Participate in workshops, volunteer opportunities, and skill-building programs.

Free — renewable annually

02

Community Member

For individuals committed to supporting NSWF's mission through active participation. Priority access to events, workshops, and networking opportunities.

€10 / year

03

Professional Member

For professionals, consultants, and academics. Includes all benefits plus eligibility for advisory panels and speaking opportunities.

€25 / year

Life Member

One-time lifetime membership. Lifetime access to all NSWF events, recognition on our website, and priority volunteer placement.
