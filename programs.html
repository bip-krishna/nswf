Our programs

Ecominds Initiative

A multi-faceted program series addressing critical environmental and social challenges through education, health, and empowerment.

Three pillars of
the initiative

01

Ecominds@Education

School and college workshops integrating environmental awareness into the curriculum. Over 27 sessions conducted across India.

27+ sessions nationwide
02

Ecominds@Health

Highlighting noise pollution's impact on physical and mental health, with guidance on protective measures.

Health awareness
03

Ecominds@Empowerment

Training programs equipping women with knowledge and skills for environmental leadership in their communities.

Women in leadership
Key activities

How we create impact

Noise Pollution Awareness

Interactive sessions educating about harmful effects of noise and promoting responsible sound practices.

Real-Time Noise Monitoring

Using sound level meters to collect decibel data during festivals and traffic hours. Collaborated with GIPE during Ganesh Utsav 2025.

E-Waste Management Drives

Scientific e-waste disposal awareness in collaboration with Electrofine Recycling Pvt. Ltd.

Water Conservation

Evaluation of rainwater harvesting systems at educational institutions to promote sustainable water management.
