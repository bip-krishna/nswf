Our Programs

Ecominds Initiative

A multi-faceted program series addressing critical environmental and social challenges through education, health, and empowerment.

01

Ecominds@Education

School and college workshops that integrate environmental awareness into the curriculum, fostering eco-consciousness from an early age. Over 27 sessions conducted across India.

02

Ecominds@Health

Highlighting the direct impact of noise pollution on physical and mental health — including hearing loss, sleep disorders, and cardiovascular risks — with guidance on protective measures.

03

Ecominds@Women Empowerment

Training and awareness programs designed to equip women with knowledge and skills for environmental leadership in their communities.

Key Activities

How We Create Impact

☬

Noise Pollution Awareness

Interactive sessions in schools, colleges, and community settings to educate about the harmful effects of noise and promote responsible sound practices.

⚙

Real-Time Noise Monitoring

Using sound level meters to collect decibel data during festivals, traffic hours, and public events. Collaborated with GIPE during Ganesh Utsav 2025.

♻

E-Waste Management Drives

Awareness programs on scientific e-waste disposal in collaboration with Electrofine Recycling Pvt. Ltd., reaching students and local communities.

☕

Water Conservation

Evaluation of rainwater harvesting systems at educational institutions to promote sustainable water management practices.
