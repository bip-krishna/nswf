Our projects

Active & Completed Projects

NSWF runs and supports a diverse portfolio of projects addressing noise pollution, environmental sustainability, education, and community well-being.

Active projects

Currently Underway

01

Pune Noise Mapping Project

Active — 2025–2026

Deploying sound level meters at 15+ strategic locations across Pune to create a comprehensive noise pollution map. Partner: GIPE, Inditronics Pvt. Ltd.

02

School Outreach Programme

Active — 2024–2026

Ongoing series of noise pollution and environmental awareness workshops at schools across Maharashtra. Over 27 sessions completed with 10,000+ students reached.

03

Women in Environmental Leadership

Active — 2025–2026

Training and mentorship program equipping women with knowledge and skills to lead environmental initiatives in their communities.

04

E-Waste Awareness & Collection Drives

Active — 2026

In collaboration with Electrofine Recycling Pvt. Ltd., NSWF conducts e-waste awareness sessions and collection drives at colleges and residential societies.

Completed projects

Past Initiatives

Ganesh Utsav Noise Monitoring 2025

Completed — September 2025

Collaboration with GIPE to measure real-time noise levels across Pune during Ganesh Utsav. Data contributed to an academic research paper.

Rainwater Harvesting Evaluation

Completed — 2025

Evaluated rainwater harvesting systems at educational institutions in Pune to promote sustainable water management practices.

Foundation Day at Orphanage

Completed — 2024

Celebrated NSWF Foundation Day and Independence Day at a local orphanage, combining environmental awareness with community engagement.

Regional Office Inauguration

Completed — December 2024

Establishment of NSWF's first regional office in Pune featuring eco-friendly infrastructure and sustainable design.
